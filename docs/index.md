# Privacy Policy for CB Interaction Viewer

This Privacy Policy describes how **CB Interaction Viewer** (the "App") handles user information.

---

## 1. Information Collection

The App **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user-identifiable data**.

Specifically, the App does **not** collect any of the following:

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal information  
- Device identifiers (such as IDFA or UUID)  
- Location information  
- Usage history or interaction logs  
- Crash reports  
- Analytics or tracking data  

---

## 2. App Functionality and Local Communication

The App is a developer tool that visualizes real-time communication between BLE devices and a mobile device simulator running on a PC, helping developers efficiently build and debug applications or devices..

Communication between the mobile device simulator app and CB Interaction Viewer is performed entirely within the local environment using **local TCP/IP communication via the loopback interface (127.0.0.1)**.  
Only technical data required for CoreBluetooth operations is exchanged.

---

## 3. Use of Bluetooth

The App uses **macOS CoreBluetooth** to perform Bluetooth communication on behalf of the iPhone Simulator, which cannot perform actual Bluetooth communication.

The information handled through Bluetooth is limited to the **minimum technical data required to establish and simulate Bluetooth communication**.  
No personal information or user-identifiable data is included.

---

## 4. External Communication

The App **does not communicate with any external servers**.

All communication and data processing performed by the App are completed entirely within the user's local environment.

---

## 5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

The App **does not share any information with third parties**.

---

## 6. Use of Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use any third-party services, including but not limited to:

- Advertising services  
- Analytics services  
- Crash reporting services  
- Tracking SDKs
